# 🚀 QUICK START - Oracle AI

## What You Can Do Now

The Oracle AI is fully integrated! You can now ask natural language questions about your simulation and get intelligent insights.

## Start the Web App

```bash
python3 web_app.py
```

Then open: **http://localhost:5001**

## How to Use Oracle AI

### Step 1: Create a Simulation
1. Enter a society description (e.g., "High school during finals week")
2. Set population (50-100 recommended)
3. Click "GENERATE & GO"

### Step 2: Ask Questions

Once the simulation is running, scroll down in the right panel to find the **🔮 Oracle AI** section.

#### Quick Queries (Click the Buttons)
- **⚠️ Most at Risk**: Find agents in danger
- **📊 Trends**: Analyze mental health patterns  
- **💼 Role Analysis**: Compare stress by role
- **💡 Interventions**: Get recommendations

#### Custom Queries (Type Your Own)
Type any question in the input box and press Enter:

```
"Who is most stressed right now?"
"What percentage of students are thriving?"
"Why are teachers struggling?"
"What interventions would help?"
"Show me agents in crisis"
"Which role has the highest anxiety?"
```

## What Data You Can Get

The Oracle AI can tell you:

### 📊 Population Statistics
- Mental health distribution (thriving, coping, struggling, crisis)

### 👥 Individual Agents

### 💼 Role Analysis
- Compare roles (teachers vs students)
- Which roles are most stressed

### 💡 Insights & Recommendations
- Why certain groups are struggling

- Patterns and trends

## Response Format

The Oracle will give you:
- **Answer**: Direct response to your question
- **Statistics**: Numbers and percentages
- **Insights**: Patterns it noticed
- **Recommendations**: What to do
- **Agents of Interest**: Specific people to watch

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Specific**: "Who are the top 3 most stressed teachers?" is better than "Who's stressed?"

2. **Ask for Numbers**: "What percentage..." gets quantitative answers

3. **Request Explanations**: "Why is..." gets deeper analysis

4. **Combine Questions**: "Who is at risk and what would help them?"

## Troubleshooting

### "AI service temporarily unavailable"
- The API might be overloaded
- Wait 10-30 seconds and try again
- The system will automatically try different models

### Slow Responses
- First query takes 5-10 seconds (cold start)
- Subsequent queries are faster (2-5 seconds)
- This is normal

### Generic Answers
- Make your question more specific
- Include role names or categories
- Ask for specific metrics
