## 🎯 Key Differences from MiroFish

### Purpose Shift
- **MiroFish**: Predict social trends and public opinion
- **MentalHealthSim**: Model therapeutic interventions and mental health trajectories

### Safety Requirements
- **MiroFish**: General content moderation
- **MentalHealthSim**: Crisis detection, bias monitoring, clinical validation

### User Base
- **MiroFish**: General users, researchers, decision-makers
- **MentalHealthSim**: Licensed professionals, supervised students, researchers

### Validation
- **MiroFish**: Accuracy against real-world events
- **MentalHealthSim**: Alignment with clinical research and expert review

### Ethical Constraints
- **MiroFish**: Standard AI ethics
- **MentalHealthSim**: Medical ethics, professional standards, safety-critical design

## 🌟 Final Thoughts

This is an ambitious and meaningful project. Mental health is complex, nuanced, and deeply personal. Technology can support human expertise, but never replace it.

### Remember:
- **Start small** - One scenario, thoroughly validated
- **Safety first** - Every single time
- **Clinical expertise** - Essential, not optional
- **Humility** - Acknowledge limitations openly
- **Iteration** - Continuous improvement based on feedback
- **Purpose** - Serve those who serve others

### You're Building:
- A training tool for future clinicians
- A research platform for better interventions
- An educational resource for understanding
- A bridge between technology and healing

### You're NOT Building:
- A replacement for therapy
- A diagnostic tool
- A self-help app
- A commercial product (at least not initially)
